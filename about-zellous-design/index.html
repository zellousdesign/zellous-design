Passionate About Helping Businesses

Zellous Design was started with the goal of helping small businesses with their online presence. Small business are the life-blood of our country. Over the years I've seen many small businesses struggle with their online presence. Zellous Design is geared towards creating websites that are easy for small businesses owners to manage.

Build Trust

When customers are researching companies to do business with, they're constantly making judgement calls. "Do they look trustworthy? Will they provide quality service/product?". Having a presentable site helps build trust with potential customers.

Update Content

Too often businesses don't update their website because the backend is intimidating. With in-page editing, making changes is simple and straightforward.

Brandon Zell - Founder and Web Designer

10 years ago I began my foray into web design while working in the FDA's recruitment department. We had several web pages needing... help. With a slow computer and a desire to learn, I started improving the web pages.

Fast forward 10 years: I now have a B.S. in Web Technology and Design, worked as an in-house marketer / designer for five years, and tackled numerous side projects. In 2015 I quit my job and started a web design company: Thus Zellous Design was born.
