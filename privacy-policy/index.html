I'm watching you Wazowski. Always watching...

Zellous Design tracks visitor information to understand how people are finding this site, how well it's meeting their needs, and if there are any code bugs that need squashing.

If you'd rather not have information related to your visit tracked, here's how you can opt-out:

Google Analytics:
Opt-out using their opt-out browser extension.

Heap Analytics:
Opt-out using the Ghostery browser extension.

Hotjar Insights:
Opt-out by turning on "Do Not Track" in your browser.
